# PhotoDay Logos

## Official Logo Kit
Download the full kit (EPS + PNG in all variants) from:
**https://media.photoday.io/resources/photoday_logo_kit.zip**

## Usage Rules (from brand-guidelines page)
- Minimum clear area: **0.3 inches** around the logo on all sides
- Minimum height: **0.5 inches** for legibility
- Always use the provided files — do not recreate or alter the logo

## What NOT to do
- Do not change the font
- Do not separate characters
- Do not remove the "smile" element
- Do not add eyes or other elements
- Do not reduce opacity
- Do not distort proportions
- Do not blur
- Do not change colors
- Do not overlay text on the logo
- Do not rotate or flip
